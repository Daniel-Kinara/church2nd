About Us

Our story, mission, and leadership

Our Story

Grace Community Church was founded in 1985 by a small group of believers who desired to create a church that would faithfully teach God's Word, love people deeply, and serve the community effectively.

From our humble beginnings meeting in a school cafeteria, we've grown to become a vibrant community of believers impacting our city and beyond for Christ.

Our journey has been marked by God's faithfulness through every season, and we continue to trust Him for our future.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ who love God, love people, and serve the world.

Our Vision

To see our city transformed by the Gospel through a growing community of fully-devoted followers of Christ.

Our Values

Biblical Truth, Authentic Community, Passionate Worship, Radical Generosity, and Compassionate Service.
