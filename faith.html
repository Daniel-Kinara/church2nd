Statement of Faith

Our core beliefs and foundational doctrines

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, authoritative Word of God. It is our final authority in all matters of faith and practice.

God

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ

We believe in the deity of Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, sinless life, miracles, atoning death, bodily resurrection, and personal return.

Salvation

We believe that salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone, not by works.

The Church

We believe in the universal church, composed of all believers, and the local church as God's primary instrument for ministry.

Second Coming

We believe in the personal, visible return of Jesus Christ to establish His kingdom and judge the living and the dead.

The Great Commission

We believe in the Great Commission to make disciples of all nations, baptizing them and teaching them to obey everything Jesus commanded.
